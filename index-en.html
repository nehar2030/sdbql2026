About the Congress

SDBQL 2026

An international platform dedicated to sustainable development and quality of life

The Second International Congress on Sustainable Development for a Better Quality of Life (SDBQL 2026) is a multidisciplinary event dedicated to the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and quality of life improvement.

Building on the success of the first edition in 2024, SDBQL 2026 aims to bring together researchers, practitioners, students, decision-makers, and all stakeholders interested in the major challenges of sustainable development.

Research Areas

Thematic Areas

Ten major themes aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals

Axis 1

Territorial and Social Development

Sustainable planning and management of territories, urban and rural planning, cultural heritage enhancement, vulnerable populations, regional development.

Axis 2

Energy, Environment, and Climate Change

Energy transition, renewable energies, climate change mitigation and adaptation, air quality, waste and wastewater management.

Axis 3

Water Resources and Agriculture

Water conservation, sustainable irrigation, food security, agro-ecology, water management, arid and semi-arid zone agriculture.

Axis 4

Natural Resources and Biodiversity

Ecosystem protection, conservation and sustainable use of natural resources, biological diversity, ecosystem services, reforestation.

Axis 5

Health, Well-being, and Sports

Physical and mental health, healthy living, sports and physical activities, mental health, preventive medicine, accessible healthcare.

Axis 6

Education and Training

Education for sustainable development (ESD), quality education, vocational training, innovative pedagogies, educational technologies.

Axis 7

Economy and Sustainable Governance

Circular economy, corporate social responsibility, green finance, sustainable governance, social entrepreneurship.

Axis 8

Innovation and Technology

Green technologies, artificial intelligence for sustainable development, digital transition, green entrepreneurship, responsible innovation.

Axis 9

Culture, Arts, and Heritage

Cultural heritage valorization, cultural industries, intercultural dialogue, art and sustainable development, living culture and traditions.

Axis 10

International Cooperation and Partnerships

South-South cooperation, international partnerships, development aid, technology transfer, academic and scientific collaboration.

The first edition of SDBQL, held in November 2024 at the University of Tlemcen, brought together over 400 participants from more than 20 countries. This success demonstrated the relevance of the topic and the need for a regular platform for exchange on sustainable development.

The presentations covered a wide range of themes, from sustainable agriculture to renewable energies, including social innovations and public policies. The debates were rich and the collaborations initiated during the event continue to bear fruit.

The congress will take place at the University of Tlemcen, a renowned higher education institution in Algeria. The modern and well-equipped facilities will provide an ideal setting for presentations, exchanges, and networking.

About Tlemcen

Tlemcen, nicknamed the "Pearl of the Maghreb," is a historic city with exceptional cultural and architectural heritage. Located in northwest Algeria, it combines modernity and authenticity, offering visitors a unique experience combining history, culture, and hospitality.
