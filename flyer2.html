About the Congress

With four years left until the 2030 deadline, only 18% of SDGs are on track, while temperatures exceed +1.55 °C and 2.3 billion people remain food insecure. The 2nd International Congress SDBQL 2026 takes place at this pivotal moment to advance this critical agenda.

Rooted in a systemic and transdisciplinary approach, the congress explores the resource nexus — water, energy, food, land — as a unifying framework, highlighting green innovation, mineral security, and inclusive governance.
